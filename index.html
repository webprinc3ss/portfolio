About Me

Welcome to my passion!

My name is Danielle and my portfolio is a representation of my 2020 journey through learning the latest techniques in web development. The web is an ever evolving medium and so are all the skills involved. What I love about working on the web is that it continues to challenge my creativity and intellect.

The sites depicted on this page show how my skills are evolving over time.

My personal philosophy about web design is that is a complex combination of diverse parts brought together, which, if done well, appears easy, simple and clean even if below the surface exist a series of complex moving parts.

My arsenal of web technologies over the years has evolved from the beginnings of HTML, Adobe Flash, DHTML, XML, JavaScript 1.0, ColdFusion to now learning modern JavaScript, JQuery, CSS3, CSS libraries such as BootStrap and more. Much of my focus has been on maintenance and management of a major non-profit website over the past ten years, but I am ready to grow my portfolio again - stay tuned!

Much of my experience is practical on-the-job experience, with focus on overall website managment and implementing the solutions that fit the situation, whether it be content, graphical, code-enhancements, CMS management, Google Ad Manager, SEO and social media integration.
